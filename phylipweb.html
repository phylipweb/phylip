PHYLIP on the Web

Documentation in HTML

Version 3.6

The documentation for PHYLIP version 3.6 is distributed in the form of web pages. The latest version

may be read here

The web pages are also included in the downloadable files.

Here are other places where the PHYLIP 3.6 web pages (or 3.61, 3.62, etc.) also available on the web:

  • The Environmental Genomics Thematic Programme Data Centre in Swindon, U.K.
  • PHYLIP documentation is available on the web site of the BIRCH package by Brian Fristensky in the University of Calgary, Alberta, Canada. Some of it is also linked to from a web tutorial on molecular phylogeny there.

Version 3.5

Quite a few sites formerly made PHYLIP v3.5 documentation available in HTML form. Many have since upgraded to version 3.6. The remaining ones include:

  • The Bioinformatics Resource at Stanford University.
  • Instituto Oswaldo Cruz, Saõ Paulo, Brazil.
  • The Bioinformatics Resource of the University of Hong Kong, China.
  • Jun Ishikawa of the National Institute of Infectious Diseases, Japan.
